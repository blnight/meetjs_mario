How to structure an app?

  • no official opinion
  • we choose ngBoilerplate
  • other boilerplates are e.g. ngTailor and ngKickstart

ngBoilerplate benefits

  • Grunt, Bower, Karma runner
  • htm2js, ngMin (now ngAnnotate), ui-router
  • business-oriented app structure
